Dispute Resolution

In order to foster a harmonious relationship between the parties through the sustenance of an alternative dispute resolution culture, the Parties hereby agree to first amicably settle and resolve

all disputes arising from or in connection with this Agreement by a process of mediation under the Lagos Multi-Door Courthouse (LMDC) Mediation Rules.

Any party may initiate mediation by giving notice to the other party or parties to the dispute, specifying the subject of the dispute. Within 14 (fourteen) days of the receipt of such notice, the other party or parties shall agree on the mediator, failing which, the mediator shall be appointed by the LMDC.

If the Parties are unable to resolve the dispute through mediation, the dispute shall be finally settled by arbitration under the Rules of the Lagos Multi-Door Courthouse (LMDC) by one or more arbitrators appointed in accordance with the said Rules.

Arbitration shall be conducted in Lagos, Nigeria and shall be conducted in the English language.

By signing this Agreement, the Parties waive any right they may have to a court action.

Any award rendered by the arbitrator(s) shall be final and binding on the Parties and may be enforced in any court of competent jurisdiction.
